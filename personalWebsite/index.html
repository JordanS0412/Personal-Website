I attended MS.137 for middle school and during my 6th and 7th grade summers I attended the BEAM (Bridge to Enter Advanced Mathematics) program who have since guided me in my mathematical learning and reccomended other STEM related programs and high schools with available college and AP clases. I graduated middle school in 2018 and during my middle school years I had gained an interest in art and animation as well as gaming. I would attend my first 2 years of highschool at Medgar Evers College Prepatory Scholol (MECPS) which had been reccomended to me by BEAM. After my first year it was decided that I no longer had any need for a para. I took my first AP classes in 10th grade and attended computer science which would teach me about Javascript and would set the first few stones in my coding travel. Halfway though the year my teacher reccomended All Star Code to me as a means to continue my computer science education. Intrigued I entered an application, and that acceptance email made feel great joy and leads me right to today, a proud student of ASC.
